About

Few genres of popular music are as polarizing as metal (aka "Heavy Metal"). Ever since its origins in Birmingham, England in 1968 at the hands of Black Sabbath, metal has most often been associated with disaffected young white males in industrialized Western societies from the dark and frozen cities of Scandinavia to the sun-drenched sidewalks of the Sunset Strip. Relying on a geodatabase with information on 150,000+ metal bands from over 150 countries across 50 years, courtesy of the Encyclopaedia Metallum: The Metal Archives, this GIS project seeks to challenge what we think we know about the history and geographic spread of the genre. This project displays locations and information on metal bands founded in China, Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan from 1969 to 2022.
