South Korea

South Korea's emergence from the destruction caused by the Korean War, 1950-53, has resulted in the creation of a dynamic and innovative economy and nation with an average annual rate of GDP growth of over 5% sustained over decades. As a result, much of the world knows the affluent country through its companies like Samsung, Hyundai, and LG. Like Japan, South Korea has hosted generation of US service members due to the continuing security threat from its militarized and autocratic neighbor to the north. This presence may account for some aspects of the development of the South Korean metal scene. The local scene is relatively small compared to Japan, but it is growing in spite of the over-shadowing global popularity of South Korean pop music, or K-pop.
