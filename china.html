Example of Distinctive Chinese Metal: Tang Dynasty

Established in 1988, Tang Dynasty is widely accepted to be China's first metal band. The band takes its name from the historical Tang Dynasty, 618-907 CE, an expansive empire whose borders extended along the Silk Roads into Central Asia. They are a prime example of how a global music genre can be assimilated into local culture and history. Their lyrical themes center around the historic Chinese conception of the "ideal man" who exhibits both cultural refinement, or "wen" and martial ability, or "wu." The band's lyrical content is largely based on poetry from the Tang period, reflecting "wen" while their sound and stage performance is largely classic heavy metal occasionally mixed with acoustic zithers for a class tea house aura.
